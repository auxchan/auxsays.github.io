# Free should stay useful at the end of the workflow

The real test of a free AI tool is not the homepage. It is what happens at the end of the workflow.

A product can look generous at first and still become useless when it is time to export, remove a watermark, increase resolution, or save a final file in a format you can actually use.

## What to check first

- Export limits
- Watermarks
- Resolution caps
- Commercial-use restrictions
- Daily quotas that make the tool impractical

## A better evaluation standard

Use each free tool on a real mini-project. If it fails at the final delivery step, treat it as a demo, not a workflow tool.
