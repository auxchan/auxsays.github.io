# Start with what the workflow actually needs

The goal is not to fight a platform. The goal is to understand the limit before it wastes your time.

For motion assets, the practical questions are simple:

- Can I use the file at the size I need?
- Can I keep quality high enough for the layout?
- Can I export or reuse the result without surprise restrictions?

## A safer path

If a hosted service becomes the bottleneck, build around open or self-controlled inputs where possible.

That can mean starting from your own SVGs, your own After Effects exports, or other openly licensed motion sources rather than assuming the marketplace asset is the workflow.

## Draft takeaway

Treat platform-hosted free assets as convenience, not ownership. If the asset is central to your brand system, build a workflow you control.
