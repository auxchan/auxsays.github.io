Not every “free” AI tool is actually useful. The real metric is not whether you can try it once. The real metric is whether you can produce a usable output without getting blocked at the finish line.

## What matters

- Can you export without a forced watermark surprise?
- Is the quality usable for real work?
- Can you repeat the workflow later?
- Does the tool help or just create dependency?

## Categories worth tracking

- image generation
- thumbnail and asset cleanup
- voice and transcription
- simple video enhancement
- workflow glue tools

## Editorial standard for this site

AUXSAYS will judge tools by **output honesty**, **repeatability**, and **actual leverage**, not hype.
