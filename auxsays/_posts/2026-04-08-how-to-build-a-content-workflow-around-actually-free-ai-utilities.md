The goal is not to glue together twenty tools just because they are free. The goal is to build a repeatable system that survives next month.

## A sane stack

1. one capture source
2. one writing or planning layer
3. one asset-generation lane
4. one publishing checklist

## Where people go wrong

- too many overlapping tools
- no naming standards
- no folder structure
- no idea which step is the real bottleneck

## What to optimize first

- time to first publishable draft
- ability to replace a failing tool quickly
- minimum manual cleanup after export

That is how free tools become real leverage instead of hobby clutter.
