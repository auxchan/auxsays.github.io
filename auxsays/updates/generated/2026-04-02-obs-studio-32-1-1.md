## TL;DR

OBS Studio 32.1.1 is the current official release listed by OBS, following the larger 32.1 release that introduced a major Audio Mixer overhaul and WebRTC support.

## Why this matters

If you stream, record, or capture content with OBS, this is the version line you should be checking first before troubleshooting workflow changes, plugin compatibility, or behavior differences between older guides and the current app.

## What changed in the 32.1 line

- The official OBS news page says OBS Studio 32.1 introduced a large Audio Mixer overhaul.
- The same release also added WebRTC support.
- OBS's official download page currently lists version 32.1.1 as the active release for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## Practical takeaway

- If your current OBS layout or audio workflow feels different than older tutorials, the 32.1 Audio Mixer overhaul is the first thing to check.
