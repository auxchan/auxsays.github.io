release_summary: "Cost attribution across your enterprise can now follow your team structure. You can add enterprise teams\
  \ as resources in a cost center, and all usage incurred by team members is attributed to that cost center. As membership\
  \ changes, whether through manual updates or identity provider (IdP) sync via SCIM, attribution updates automatically, with\
  \ no reassignment required.\n\n\n Enterprise teams organize who belongs to a cost center. Budgets and usage caps attach\
  \ to the cost center itself, so adding a team is how you keep membership current, not how you set spending limits.\n\n\n\
  \ This is especially useful for enterprises that already use enterprise teams to manage access and roles across organizations.\
  \ You can use those same teams to drive cost attribution, keeping your billing structure aligned with your org structure.\
  \ It is also the foundation for capping spend by group: once a team is in a cost center, you can set a budget on that cost\
  \ center and every member inherits it.\n\n\n Who can use this feature\n Enterprise owners and billing managers on GitHub\
  \ Enterprise Cloud.\n\n\n To get started, go to your enterprise’s Billing and licensing settings, open Cost centers , and\
