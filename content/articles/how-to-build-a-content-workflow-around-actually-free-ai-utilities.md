## Build around swap-ability

The strongest free-tool workflow is not one perfect app. It is a system where each step can be replaced without collapsing the whole project.

## Suggested stack model

1. Input capture
2. Draft generation
3. Cleanup and refinement
4. Packaging and distribution

## Why this matters

If one app suddenly becomes paid, watermarked, or limited, a modular workflow survives. A locked stack does not.
